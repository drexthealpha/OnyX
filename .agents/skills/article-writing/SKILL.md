# Article Writing

Write long-form content that carries the author's genuine perspective, not generic AI-generated text.

## When to Activate

- Writing articles, essays, or technical posts
- Creating documentation or tutorials
- Drafting blog content or opinion pieces
- Producing any substantive text that should feel human-authored

## Core Principles

### 1. Lead with a Concrete Artifact
Open with a specific example, data point, code snippet, or observation. Never start with a vague generalstatement.

### 2. Maintain Author Voice
Your writing should sound like one person with a point of view. If multiple voices are present (like a publication), match that publication's style guide.

### 3. Structure for Scannability
Use clear headings, short paragraphs, and strategic formatting. Writers often read first, browsers scan.

### 4. Specificity Beats Adjectives
Replace vague modifiers with concrete details. Instead of "recently improved," say "improved 40% in the last quarter."

## Hard Bans — Delete These Phrases

- "In today's rapidly evolving landscape"
- "game-changer" or "revolutionary"
- "cutting-edge"
- "here's why this matters" without immediate evidence
- Any paragraph that exists only to fill space

## Structural Template

```markdown
# [Concrete, Specific Title]

## The Hook
Open with a specific moment, number, or artifact — not a general statement.

## The Argument
Present your thesis with clear supporting evidence. One claim per paragraph.

## The Evidence
Include data, code, examples, or citations. Make every claim supportable.

## The Implications
What does this mean for the reader? What's the call to action?

## The Close
End with a specific forward-looking statement or question — not a summary.
```

## Voice Matching

When adapting someone else's voice:
1. Read 5+ pieces they've written
2. Note their sentence length preference, vocabulary, and rhetorical patterns
3. Match their rhythm and level of formality
4. Never add enthusiasm they wouldn't express

## Examples

```
BAD: "In today's rapidly evolving AI landscape, there are many challenges."

GOOD: "Three days into debugging, I found the problem: a race condition that only appeared when the API received 50 concurrent requests."
```
